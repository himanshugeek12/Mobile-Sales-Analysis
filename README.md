# Mobile-Sales-Analysis (Interactive Dashboard creation using Power BI & SQL)

## Project Objective
The objective of the Mobile Sales Analysis Dashboard is to analyze sales performance, transaction trends, customer behavior, and product demand across different cities, brands, and payment methods. This dashboard helps businesses understand key revenue drivers and optimize sales strategies.

## Dataset used 
- <a href="https://github.com/himanshugeek12/Mobile-Sales-Analysis/blob/master/Mobile-Sales-Analysis.pbix">Dataset</a>

## ❓ Key Questions (KPIs)
- What is the **total sales revenue generated**?
- What is the **total quantity of products sold**?
- How many **total transactions** were recorded?
- What is the **average sales value per transaction**?
- Which **cities contribute the highest sales**?
- What is the **monthly trend of total quantity sold**?
- How do **different payment methods contribute to transactions**?
- What are the **customer rating distributions**?
- Which **mobile models generate the highest sales**?
- What is the **day-wise sales performance trend**?

 - Dashboard Interaction <a href="https://github.com/himanshugeek12/Mobile-Sales-Analysis/blob/master/Mobile-Sales-Analysis.png">View Dashboard</a>

## 🔄 Process
- Collected and validated raw data using SQL queries.
- Cleaned and transformed data using Power Query.
- Created calculated measures using DAX such as:
  - Total Sales  
  - Total Quantity  
  - Total Transactions  
  - Average Sales  
- Built interactive dashboard visuals including:
  - Sales by city (map visualization)  
  - Monthly sales trend (line chart)  
  - Payment method distribution (pie chart)  
  - Customer ratings (bar chart)  
  - Top-performing mobile models (bar chart)  
  - Day-wise sales trend  
- Applied slicers for dynamic filtering (Month, Mobile Model, Payment Method, Brand, City).

## 🔍 Project Insights
- **Total sales reached 769M**, indicating strong overall business performance.
- **Total quantity sold is 19K**, with **4K transactions** recorded.
- **Average transaction value is around 40K**, showing high-value purchases.
- **UPI and Debit Cards** are the most preferred payment methods.
- Certain **cities dominate sales contribution**, highlighting key markets.
- **Sales peak observed in March and July**, indicating seasonal demand trends.
- **iPhone SE and OnePlus models** are among the top-performing products.
- Customer ratings are mostly **4 and 5**, indicating high satisfaction levels.

## ✅ Final Conclusion  
The **Mobile Sales Analysis Dashboard** provides valuable insights into sales trends, customer preferences, and product performance. It enables businesses to make data-driven decisions by identifying top-performing products, optimizing sales channels, and improving customer experience.
